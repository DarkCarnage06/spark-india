About Indo Science Education Trust (ISET)

Indo Science Education Trust (ISET) is an authorized ISRO Space Tutor organization committed to transforming STEM education through experiential learning, innovation, and real-world scientific exposure.

The SPARK INDIA Program is a joint educational initiative by Indo Science Education Trust (ISET) and ISRO–SPPU Space Technology Cell (STC), designed to inspire and empower the next generation of scientists, innovators, and space technologists.

Working in alignment with the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and ISRO–SPPU Space Technology Cell (STC), ISET has impacted more than 20 lakh students across India through hands-on science programs, innovation initiatives, astronomy workshops, and space technology education.

Our mission is to make advanced scientific learning accessible, practical, and inspiring for students across urban, rural, and semi-urban India.

Key Experiential Components

1. Satellite Building

Students develop miniature educational satellites integrated with sensors and telemetry systems.

2. Telescope Making &amp; Astronomy

Hands-on telescope assembly and practical astronomy observation sessions.

3. Rocketry &amp; Propulsion

Exposure to rocket aerodynamics, propulsion systems, and launch principles.

4. IoT Payload Integration

Practical implementation of GPS tracking, coding, sensors, and real-time data systems.

5. Aryabhatta 50-Year Tribute Initiative

A national-level educational satellite activity honoring India’s first satellite mission and inspiring future innovation in space technology.
