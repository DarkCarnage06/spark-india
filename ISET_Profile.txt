About ISET: Pioneering Space Education Excellence

Indo Science Education Trust (ISET) stands at the forefront of India’s space education revolution as an authorized ISRO Space Tutor and Official Merchandise Partner.

We deliver transformative STEM education through hands-on innovation, research exposure, and real-world applications, bridging the gap between classroom learning and India’s rapidly advancing space ecosystem.

From Student-built Satellites to National-level Space Science &amp; Tech Exhibitions, ISET has empowered over 20,00,000+ students across India—nurturing curiosity, innovation, and scientific temperament aligned with NEP 2020.

Aligned with the ISRO–SPPU Space Technology Cell, ISET enables learners from Grades 1–10 and beyond to explore cutting-edge domains such as space science, robotics, AI, and satellite technology.

3. Rocketry &amp; Aeromodelling
